OUR HISTORY

CABSEAL was founded in 1993 and the family business established their own production early on. The company was among the first in business holding a ISO-certificate and has successfully developed into a company with a triple-A credit rating. The environmental perspective is important to Cabseal which consciously works with reducing material consumption.

 

With us you benefit from

• A total supplier with a wide assortment
• Professional and wide knowledgement
• Great customer service with focus on quality and short lead times
• Cost-effective products
• A well established company with professional raw material suppliers
• Competitive prices
• A flexible company with short lines

INTRODUCING WATERJET

We’re proud to present the latest addition to our machining capailites – WATERJET. This investment enables us to expand our range of gaskets, seals and insulations to meet our customers’ needs. We can now water-cut all rubber and plastics up to 100mm thickness and adding several new cutting heads to our range allows us to cut up to 4 meters in length.
